About CardCollectables

At CardCollectables, we have a huge passion for Pokemon cards. Since 2019, collecting has been more than a hobby - it's our way of life. We're completely cardboard mad, and it shows in everything we do.

Our Story

What started as a shared interest quickly became an all-consuming collection. We've spent years building our knowledge, understanding card values, and learning every detail about grading, condition, and market trends. We've hunted for rare finds, celebrated big pulls, and invested countless hours in this community we love.

That deep knowledge and genuine enthusiasm is what drives CardCollectables. We understand what collectors value because we're collectors ourselves. We know the excitement of finding hidden gems and the care required to preserve them properly.

Why We Started This

We noticed a gap in how trading cards were being handled. Sometimes with pressure, sometimes with unclear valuations, and often without genuine care. We decided to bring our passion into a business that reflects our values: Transparency, fairness, and respect for every collection regardless of value.

When you work with us, you're working with real collectors who understand your cards. We evaluate every collection thoughtfully, provide honest assessments, and handle each piece with the respect it deserves.

What We Do

We purchase Pokemon card and collections of all sizes, grades, and eras. Whether you've got a handful of commons or a portfolio of vintage holographics, we approach every submission with the same level of care and attention. We buy your unwanted items in bulk, so you don't have to worry about individually selling.

Our goal is simple: Create a trustworthy, professional experience where both sellers and buyers feel genuinely valued. Because at the end of the day, we're all in this for the same reason - we love trading cards, and we want to share that passion with others.

Thanks for being part of our journey.

- CardCollectables
